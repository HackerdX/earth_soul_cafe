About Earth Soul Cafe

Located in the heart of CBD Belapur, Navi Mumbai, Earth Soul Cafe is your destination for mindful, delicious dining. We specialize in fresh, healthy, plant-based cuisine that doesn't compromise on flavor. Our menu features an artful fusion of Indian, European, and Italian dishes, all crafted with health and sustainability in mind.

From vibrant smoothie bowls packed with superfoods to wholesome gluten-free pizzas topped with fresh ingredients, every dish is designed to nourish both body and soul. Our cozy, eco-conscious café provides the perfect retreat for health-conscious food lovers, offering a serene atmosphere where you can enjoy nutritious meals in a welcoming environment.
